Berlin Tech Map

Did you know there are over 1800 active startup companies operating in Berlin? To discover more about them, we took a 10% sample sorted them by industry and mapped them by U-bahn station. The following post details our findings.

According to EY, in 2015 growth in Germany outpaced other European tech regions such as the UK, Sweden or France. With Berlin centered at the beating heart of this growth and home to roughly 1,800 active tech statups, analysts estimate that the city's startups could create between 40,000 and 100,000 new jobs by 2020.
